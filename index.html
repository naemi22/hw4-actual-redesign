Exhibition: EMOP Berlin What Stands Between Us. Photography as a Medium for Chronicling
Fr 28.2
Mon 04.5

In times of crisis, we let ourselves be polarised by images. We want to use our own view – and images – to counter the increasing division. But what meaning can photographic images still convey? Isn’t it the camera that stands between us? This central festival exhibition of EMOP Berlin – European Month of Photography 2025 presents projects by around 20 artists that stand for listening and learning from Others through their own voices.
